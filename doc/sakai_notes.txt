Setting up the BLTI tool in Sakai
---------------------------------

1. Go to https://nd-test.rsmart.com/xsl-portal
2. Click "Guest Login"
3. Input the "hesburgh" user account credentials
4. Click on "Administration Workspace" at the top
5. Click on "Site Setup" in the left hand column
6. Search for the course site you want using the right most search box. There are several methods you can try. The most accurate would be to use the term-alpha-number course title as a search term. For example, to find the Fall 2013 course entitled "Comedy, Italian Style Lab", you could type in FA13-LLRO-41542, and this will bring up the course.
7. Click on the "Worksite Title" for the course site.
